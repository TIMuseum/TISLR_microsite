About Treasure Island Museum

The Treasure Island Museum is funded by individuals and some foundations. We are grateful for your contribution to our work. Contributions go to program development, curriculum development and presenting the stories of Treasure Island and their outsized impact to the burgeoning Bay Area and its many visitors.
